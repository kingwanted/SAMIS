About Samis

About Us

Samis Softwares Limited is a Kenyan-based IT Consultancy company,founded in 2013.It specializes in the development of softwares for schools and other organizations.We are the developers of;
School Management Information System(SAMIS), a system that manages the school's Academics, Financial data,and also sends Bulk SMS,
Judiciary Financial Management Information System(JFMIS), a system used by the Judicial commission of the Republic of Kenya,
School Inventory Information System(SIMIS),a system to manage a school's inventory.
Among others.

Vision

To be the leading Software solution company for schools and other organizations.We understand that the satisfaction of our clients translates to our growth,hence the reason why we are committed to providing quality systems and superb customer support.

Mission

To help schools and other institutions with management by providing reliable, user-friendly,feature-rich and effective management softwares.
